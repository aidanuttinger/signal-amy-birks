LinkedIn DM Cold outreach

Why this works
Opens with insight, not credentials — the reader immediately wonders "what risk factor?"
Reframes the problem in their language — "protecting synergy assumptions" is CFO language
The calculator is the CTA, not a call — lower friction, creates warm lead before conversation
